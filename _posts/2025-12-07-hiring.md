The field of <i>Machine Learning on Graphs</i> aims to extract knowledge from graph-structured and network data through machine learning models. LOGSMS will combine diverse tools from discrete mathematics, learning theory and machine learning, thus facilitating the design and analysis of <i>provably powerful</i> graph learning models.
